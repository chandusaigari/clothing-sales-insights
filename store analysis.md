**Store Analysis**

This section provides insights into store performance, revenue distribution, and customer purchase behavior across regions and channels:
<a href="https://github.com/chandusaigari/clothing-sales-insights/blob/main/store%20analysis.png">Store Analysis<a/>
**Insights**

**Revenue by Store Region**

Helps identify top-performing and underperforming regions.

Useful for regional marketing and expansion planning.

Payment Methods in Store

Shows customer preference for payment types.

Guides operational improvements (e.g., adding more UPI/Wallets if usage is high).

**Units Sold by Channel**

Measures contribution of online vs. offline (or direct vs. distributor).

Helps optimize channel strategy.

**Units Sold by Payment Mode**

Tracks purchase patterns across different payment types.

Useful for improving checkout experience and promotions
